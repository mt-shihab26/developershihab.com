# **Swiss**

## **Overview**

Swiss is a versatile collection of web tools designed to simplify your digital life and streamline everyday tasks. The platform offers a comprehensive suite of utilities that are powerful, user-friendly, and privacy-focused — all accessible in one place, completely free, with no sign-up required.

## **Features**

**Multi-Tool Platform** – Comprehensive utility collection  
**Privacy-Focused** – No data collection or tracking  
**No Registration** – Immediate access without sign-up  
**User-Friendly** – Intuitive interface design  
**Free Forever** – All tools available at no cost

## **Tech Stack**

- **Framework:** Astro
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **Styling:** TailwindCSS
