# **Stockly**

## **Overview**

Stockly is an AI-powered inventory management SaaS built with Laravel, React, and Inertia.js. It gives businesses complete visibility into their stock — tracking products across multiple warehouses, managing supplier relationships and purchase orders, and using AI to forecast demand and trigger smart reorder suggestions before stockouts happen.

## **Features**

**Product Management** – Manage products with SKUs, categories, variants, units of measure, and supplier mappings

**Multi-Warehouse Support** – Track stock levels independently per warehouse location with transfer management between locations

**Purchase Orders** – Create, send, and track purchase orders with suppliers from draft through to received, with PDF export

**Stock Movements** – Log every stock-in, stock-out, adjustment, and transfer with a full auditable history

**Supplier Management** – Maintain a supplier directory with contact details, lead times, and performance history

**Low Stock Alerts** – Configurable thresholds per product that trigger real-time notifications when stock drops below the minimum

**AI Demand Forecasting** – OpenAI analyses historical stock movement data to predict future demand and flag products at risk of stockout

**Smart Reorder Suggestions** – AI generates purchase order suggestions with recommended quantities and timing based on sales velocity and supplier lead times

**Natural Language Queries** – Ask questions like "Which products are running low this week?" and get instant AI-generated answers from your inventory data

**Dashboard Overview** – Live overview of total stock value, low stock count, pending orders, and movement activity

**Analytics & Reports** – Stock valuation reports, movement history, supplier performance, and slow-moving inventory — all exportable to PDF and Excel

**Role-Based Access** – Owner, Warehouse Manager, and Staff roles with fine-grained permissions per workspace

**Bulk Import** – Import products and opening stock from Excel/CSV to get up and running fast

## **Tech Stack**

- **Backend:** Laravel (Queues, Horizon, Scheduling, Events, Notifications, Policies)
- **Frontend:** React + Inertia.js
- **Styling:** TailwindCSS + shadcn/ui
- **AI:** OpenAI (demand forecasting, reorder suggestions, natural language queries)
- **Database:** MySQL
- **Queue Monitoring:** Laravel Horizon
- **Permissions:** Spatie Laravel Permission
- **Activity Log:** Spatie Laravel Activitylog
- **File Storage:** Laravel Storage (S3-compatible)
- **PDF:** Laravel DomPDF
- **Excel:** Maatwebsite Laravel Excel
