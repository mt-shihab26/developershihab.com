# PracticeScope

## Overview

PracticeScope is an AI-powered SaaS platform built for dentists evaluating practice acquisitions. Users upload financial documents or enter practice data manually, instantly see 30+ benchmarked diagnostic metrics, and generate a personalized $7 AI acquisition playbook — so they never overpay for a practice.

## How It Works

**Upload or Enter Data** – Drop in a P&L, tax return, or practice summary. AI extracts and populates 50+ fields automatically — no manual data entry.

**Instant Diagnostics** – See 30+ metrics covering production, overhead, collections, hygiene, and patient flow — all benchmarked against industry standards with dollar-amount upside attached.

**AI Acquisition Report** – Generate a personalized playbook with negotiation talking points, risk flags, growth strategies, and valuation modeling for $7 per report.

## Features

**30+ Diagnostic Metrics** – Comprehensive analysis benchmarked against industry standards across production, overhead, collections, hygiene, and patient flow

**AI-Powered Playbook** – Personalized acquisition report with actionable insights, negotiation talking points, and dollar-amount upside per metric

**Risk Assessment** – Identifies red flags and hidden opportunities before signing; rates every metric so buyers know exactly what they're buying

**Valuation & Loan Modeling** – Model purchase price, loan scenarios, and projected cash flow to understand true cost of ownership

**Document Upload & Parsing** – AI extracts and populates 50+ fields from P&Ls, tax returns, and practice summaries automatically

**Exportable PDF Reports** – Professional, investor-ready acquisition reports shareable with lenders, partners, and advisors

**Stripe Payments** – Pay-per-report pricing at $7 — free to explore, no subscription required

**Admin Dashboard** – Full platform visibility into users, analyses, and revenue

## Tech Stack

- **Backend:** Go + PocketBase (API, auth, file storage, admin)
- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript
- **Styling:** TailwindCSS + shadcn/ui
- **Payments:** Stripe
- **AI:** OpenAI API (document parsing, report generation)
